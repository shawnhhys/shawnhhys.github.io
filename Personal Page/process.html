Documentation

Design Process

A walkthrough of the design decisions, typology, and creative thinking behind each page of this portfolio.

Page 01

Home

Landing Page
img/index.png

Concept & Aesthetic

The home page establishes the galaxy/space theme that runs throughout the entire portfolio. A dark, deep-navy background immediately signals the color identity, while a warm gold accent creates contrast and draws attention to key elements.

The Pacifico typeface in the original greeting was chosen to catch the eye immediately — a curly, welcoming script that contrasts with the cosmic backdrop.

Structure

The page is divided into two sections: a full-screen hero greeting the visitor, followed by a card grid previewing the four main sections of the site.

  • Hero: full-viewport with background image and centered text
  • Cards: dark-background cards with gold details, each linking to a section
  • Navigation: persistent dark navbar with space-branded logo icon

Color Decisions

Black was chosen as the base color to anchor the space/galaxy theme. The icon was set in a cyan-adjacent tone in the original to provide a welcoming pop against the dark field — a color often associated with sci-fi and technology. Gold accents were used for the cards to evoke a sense of quality and contrast.

Page 02

About

Content Heavy
img/about.png

Intent

The About page needed to feel warmer and more approachable than the home page. A lighter background image was used in the original to create a more inviting, energetic feel — moving away from the cold depth of space into something more human.

Content Structure

Being primarily text-based, the About page focuses on readability and rhythm. A self-introduction video (autoplay, muted, looped) was placed at the top to give visitors an immediate personal impression before reading.

  • Video header for personality and warmth
  • Text paragraphs covering background, education, and experience
  • Highlighted links to UTM, CCIT, Art History, and work experience

Typography

Montserrat and Open Sans were used to keep things readable while maintaining a modern feel. The content hierarchy was maintained through clear paragraph structure rather than heavy use of headers.

Page 03

Design

Photo Rich
img/work.png

Purpose

This gallery page showcases past design work from another course. The primary goal is to display the work prominently without distraction, letting the images speak for themselves.

Interaction Design

Clicking any image in the grid opens a Bootstrap carousel modal — a lightbox-style experience that allows visitors to move through all works without leaving the page. The dark blue highlight in the dropdown navigation reminds users which section they are currently viewing.

  • Masonry-style grid of thumbnails
  • Click-to-expand carousel modal
  • Caption for each piece explaining its concept

Page 04

Contact

Form
img/contact.png

Function

The contact page provides a way for viewers to reach out if they are interested in collaboration or just want to connect. A tabbed layout separates the contact form from credits information, keeping the interface tidy.

Form Details

The form includes email, message, and a checkbox that optionally subscribes the user to a Bilibili channel for D&D campaign content. Client-side validation prevents empty or malformed submissions.

  • Email input with format validation
  • Textarea for freeform message
  • Bilibili subscription checkbox with icon
  • Consistent gold button color continuing the site theme
