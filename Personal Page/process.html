Design Process

A walkthrough of the design decisions, typology, and creative thinking behind each page of this portfolio.

Page 01

Home

Landing Page
img/index.png · (add screenshot of home page)

Concept & Aesthetic

The home page establishes the galaxy/space theme that runs throughout the entire portfolio. A dark, deep navy background immediately signals the color identity, while warm gold accents create contrast and draw attention to key elements.

The original greeting used a curly, welcoming script font to catch the eye immediately — a visual contrast against the cosmic backdrop.

Structure

The page is divided into two sections: a full-screen hero greeting the visitor, followed by a card grid previewing the four main sections of the site.

  • Hero: full-viewport greeting with background image and starfield effect
  • Cards: dark-background cards with gold details and image thumbnails, each linking to a section
  • Navigation: persistent dark navbar with space-branded logo

Color Decisions

Black anchors the space/galaxy theme. Gold accents evoke quality and create visual hierarchy. The original cyan accent was chosen to feel welcoming and tech-forward against the dark field.

Page 02

About

Content Heavy
img/about.png · (add screenshot of about page)

Intent

The About page balances warmth with professionalism. A self-introduction video plays at the top (autoplay, muted, looped) to give visitors an immediate personal impression before reading any text.

Content Structure

A two-column layout separates a sticky sidebar from the main narrative. The sidebar contains education, skills, interests, and links. The main content flows naturally: introduction, capabilities, experience timeline, and personal interests.

  • Video header for personality and human connection
  • Sticky sidebar with quick facts and skill tags
  • Main prose narrative and timeline
  • External links to university, programs, and past employers

Visual Hierarchy

Montserrat and Open Sans were replaced with Cormorant Garamond and DM Sans for a more sophisticated feel. Clear paragraph structure and gold accent links guide the reader through the narrative.

Page 03

Design

Photo Rich

Purpose

This gallery page showcases past design work. The primary goal is to display the work prominently without distraction, letting images speak for themselves while hover overlays provide context.

Interaction Design

A masonry-style grid displays thumbnails. Clicking any image opens a custom lightbox overlay with the full-resolution image and a descriptive caption. The layout is responsive, collapsing to a single column on mobile.

  • Masonry grid with image previews and labels
  • Click-to-expand lightbox modal with captions
  • Hover overlay showing design type and brief description
  • Keyboard support: ESC closes the lightbox

Page 04

Contact

Form
img/contact.png · (add screenshot of contact page)

Function

The contact page provides a clear, inviting way for viewers to reach out for collaboration or just to connect. A two-column layout presents contact info and quick links on the left, and a clean form on the right.

Form Details

The form captures email and message, with optional subscription to a Bilibili channel. Client-side validation prevents empty or malformed submissions. A success message displays upon valid submission (form data is not actually sent — add a backend service like Formspree if needed).

  • Email input with format validation
  • Textarea for freeform message
  • Checkbox for Bilibili subscription opt-in
  • Gold submit button matching site theme
  • Real-time error display and success feedback
