Hello, I'm Shawn

My name is Xiaoen — most people call me Shawn, which is a bit easier. I'm 21 years old, originally from China, and currently living in Mississauga to be close to campus.

I study at the University of Toronto Mississauga as an undergraduate pursuing a double major in CCIT (Communication, Culture, Information & Technology) and Art History.

Capabilities

Through coursework and independent projects I've built practical skills in front-end development (HTML, CSS), data visualization (Excel, Tableau), and Adobe Creative Suite tools including Illustrator, Photoshop, and Media Encoder. I'm also trained in image analysis and various design methodologies.
