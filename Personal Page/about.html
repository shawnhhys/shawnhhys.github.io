Hello, I'm Shawn

My name is Xiaoen — most people call me Shawn, which is a bit easier. I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto Mississauga, pursuing a double major in Communication, Culture, Information & Technology (CCIT) and Art History.

I'm originally from China and currently based in Mississauga. My studies have given me a foundation at the intersection of technology, visual culture, and human communication — a space I find endlessly fascinating.

Capabilities

Through coursework and independent projects, I've developed practical proficiency in front-end web development (HTML, CSS), data visualization (Excel, Tableau), and a range of Adobe Creative Suite tools including Illustrator, Photoshop, and Media Encoder. I'm also trained in image analysis and various design methodologies.
